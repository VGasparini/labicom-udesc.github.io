Intelligent COMputing Research Laboratory - ICOM Lab

The Intelligent Computing Research Laboratory (Laboratório de Pesquisa em Inteligência Computacional - ICOM LAb) is attached to the Graduate Programm in Applied Computing from the Santa Catarina State University (UDESC, Joinville) and is part of the Applied Cognitive Computing Group (Grupo de Computação Cognitiva Aplicada - COCA). The head of COCA and the coordinator of ICOM Lab is the prof. Rafael Stubs Parpinelli.

The main research projects developed at ICOM Lab are related to:

  • Deep Learning
  • Bioinformatics
  • Evolutionary Computation
  • Swarm Intelligence
  • Computer Vision
  • Data Mining
  • High-performance Computing
  • Multi/Many Objectives Optimization
  • Source Codes

      • PARPINELLI, R.S., LOPES, H.S. and FREITAS, A.A. "Data Mining with an Ant Colony Optimization Algorithm". In: IEEE Transactions on Evolutionary Computation, Volume 6, Issue 4, pp. 321–332. IEEE, 2002.
    • cujDE: A GPU-based jDE algorithm for unconstrained continuous optimization.
      • BOIANI, M. ; DOMINICO, G. ; PARPINELLI, R. S. . "A GPU-based jDE Algorithm Applied to Continuous Unconstrained Optimization". In: International Conference on Intelligent Systems Design and Applications, 2018.
    • jDE: A sequential implementation of a self-adaptive differential evolution namely jDE in C++.
    • SHADE: Success-History based parameter Adaptation for Differential Evolution proposed by Ryoji Tanabe and Alex Fukunaga (2013).
